How Bio-Oil works

Bio Oil contains the breakthrough ingredient PurCellin OilTM which works by reducing the thickness of the formulation making Bio Oil easier and quicker for the skin to absorb. Making it a highly effective fast acting, specialist skincare treatment that can be used to improve the appearance of:

Scars

Bio Oil is specially formulated to help improve the appearance of both new and old scars. Although Bio Oil will never remove scars it can help improve the appearance of scars through regular use. For best results it is advised that Bio Oil should be massaged in a circular motion into the scar, twice daily, for a minimum of 3 months.

Stretch Marks

Bio Oil is specially formulated to help reduce the possibility of stretch marks forming in those most likely to be effected such as pregnant women, body builders, adolescents undergoing sudden growth spurts and individuals who experience rapid weight gain. Bio Oil can also help improve the appearance of existing stretch marks although will never remove them.

Uneven Skin Tone

Bio Oil can help improve the appearance of pigmentation marks and blemishes caused by hormonal fluctuations, skin lighteners or excessive sun exposure. Bio Oil should be applied to the affected areas, twice daily, for a minimum of 3 months.

Ageing Skin

Bio Oil can help smooth and tone ageing, sagging and wrinkled skin on both the face and body.

Dehydrated Skin

Bio Oil works by helping to replenish the skins natural oils, which can be stripped away through time by factors such as extreme weather conditions, water with high chemical content, frequent bathing and the drying effects that central heating and air conditioning systems can have on your skin.
